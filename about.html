About me

Raman International Institute of Information Technology (RiiiT) is India’s first IT Finishing School (ITFS) started in 2007 with a mission to ameliorate Information & Communication Technology education in India and to churn out quality professionals for the industry. It caters to the needs of IT Industry by improving employability skills of unemployed fresh Engineering graduates and provides employment opportunities through its well designed curriculum to meet the ever growing demands of IT industry. Finishing School grooms internees not only on Technology but also on how to behave, how to interact and communicate with people. For a Fresher at RiiiT, ‘Experiential Learning’ gives the needed exposure and insight on industry culture and standards. For the corporate RiiiT has been delivering industry ready - experienced fresher or a billable resource with very little training and at absolutely no cost.

Other initiatives by RiiiT

RiiiT has developed world class learning content for more than 60 IT subjects under the business vertical K12.
Contents and assessments are developed in par with international standards like ADDIE, Bloom’s, Gagne Nine ID etc.
RiiiT’s IT Kids- Study Material, published & promoted by Cambridge University Press India, reaches more than 5,00,000 students across South Asia.
